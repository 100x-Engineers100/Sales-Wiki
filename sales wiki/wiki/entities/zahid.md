## Overview

Zahid is the CRO (Chief Revenue Officer) and co-founder of 100xEngineers. He personally gets on sales calls with leads. His call style is the gold standard for the sales team — consultative, curiosity-driven, never pushy.

## Key Facts / Role

- Role: CRO + co-founder
- Style: Consultative. Discovery-first. Makes leads reflect rather than convincing them.
- Core philosophy: "My goal is not to sell the course. My goal is to make them confront their reality. Once that happens, the sale becomes natural."
- Gets on calls himself to stay close to the sales process
- Uses the 9-step call framework (see [[call-framework]]) naturally in every call
