## Overview

Varun Mayya is the founder and CEO of Aeos Group — a bootstrapped media-tech company (500+ people, Bengaluru) that Varun built without VC. 100xEngineers operates under the Aeos umbrella. He is a prominent AI developer, entrepreneur, and one of India's most recognized voices on AI and automation.

Known for: bootstrapping a 500-person company, automating content creation at scale, being a practitioner-first builder (not just a speaker).
