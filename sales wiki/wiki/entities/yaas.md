## Overview

YaaS (YouTube as a Service) is a sister company of 100xEngineers. It manages YouTube channels for 40+ clients and has AI-automated a significant portion of its content production pipeline. Grew from a 20-person vision to 250+ employees in under a year due to AI-driven content velocity.
