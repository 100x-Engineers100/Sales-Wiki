## Definition

The technique of immediately finding something from the prospect's exact professional world and using it to make the cohort concrete and credible. Instead of generic "AI is useful everywhere" claims, the rep surfaces a specific real example — from a sister company, a past student, or a live use case — that operates in the prospect's domain.

## Why It Matters (for sales)

Prospects can smell generic pitches. "AI helps everyone" converts nobody. "AI is being used right now to [specific thing in their exact world]" creates instant credibility and emotional resonance. Zahid's Rohit call shows this working in real-time: within 2 minutes of learning Rohit runs a media agency, Zahid pulled YaaS + Sportskeeda + Unacademy examples. Rohit immediately said "we tried something similar."

## How to Use It

**Step 1:** During discovery, identify their domain clearly.

**Step 2:** Match to the closest available example:
- Known sister company use case (YaaS for media/content, AeOS Labs for analytics/operations)
- Specific past student story (see [[success-stories]])
- Real 100x use case shared publicly

**Step 3:** For domains not covered in the wiki, use Claude web search to find current AI trends in their specific field, then relate those back to what the cohort teaches.

**The formula:** "[Real example in their world] + [what module covers that] = [what they could build]"

## When to Use It (trigger)

Step 3 of the call framework — immediately after discovery reveals their domain. Don't wait until the formal pitch. Mirror as soon as you know their world.

## Examples from Calls

- Rohit (media agency): YaaS Instagram 80% automated → Sportskeeda short-form pipeline → Unacademy AI presenter
- Harsh (business analyst): AeOS Labs (sister company solving BA/operations problems for enterprises)

## Domain Mirror Bank (quick reference)

| Domain | Mirror to use |
|--------|--------------|
| Media / video production / content | YaaS (YouTube as a Service), Sportskeeda automation, AI presenter |
| Business analytics / operations | AeOS Labs (enterprise AI solutions) |
| Marketing | 100x's own Instagram (80% AI-automated), content pipeline |
| Any domain without a ready mirror | Web search current AI trends → map to cohort module |
