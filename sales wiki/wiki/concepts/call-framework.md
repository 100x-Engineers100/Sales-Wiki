## Definition

The systematic call pattern used by Zahid (CRO) across all sales calls. Nine steps from opening to handoff. Not a script — a thinking framework. Every step has a purpose. Skipping steps leads to premature pitching and lost leads.

## Why It Matters (for sales)

Most reps jump to the pitch too early. This framework ensures the rep understands the prospect's world before presenting anything, making every pitch feel personalized and relevant rather than generic.

## How to Use It

**Step 1: Permission Opening**
Always ask before taking time. "Want to take two minutes of your time?"
Never assume they're ready to talk.

**Step 2: Discovery First**
Before any pitch, understand: current role, AI intent (career growth or general curiosity?), where they want to be in 6-12 months.
DO NOT pitch until you have these answers. The pitch changes based on what they say.

**Step 3: Domain Mirroring**
Find something from their exact world. Connect it to 100x immediately.
- Media agency → YaaS, Sportskeeda, AI presenter
- Business analyst → AeOS Labs
- Marketing → Instagram automation, content pipelines
- Any domain → web search for current AI trends in their field

**Step 4: Experience Reframe**
Use on anyone with 3+ years experience. See [[experience-reframe]].

**Step 5: FOMO — IT Wave vs AI Wave**
Use after rapport is built. Never as an opener. See [[it-wave-ai-wave]].

**Step 6: Matched Success Story**
Never generic. Always pick the story closest to their world. See [[matched-success-story]].

**Step 7: Cohort Pitch (brief)**
Only after steps 1-6. Key points: 6 months, 4 modules, Fri/Sat + office hours, 8-10 hrs/week, build real projects from your own problems.

**Step 8: Handle Objections**
Time → learn-on-real-problems reframe. Price → ROI reframe + clear disclosure. Relevance → domain mirroring + success story (already done in steps 3+6).

**Step 9: Never Close on Call 1**
End with: "I'll have [rep] send you the brochure and details on WhatsApp. We'll follow up in a couple of days."

## When to Use It (trigger)

Every outbound call to a lead who attended a webinar or filled a lead form.
