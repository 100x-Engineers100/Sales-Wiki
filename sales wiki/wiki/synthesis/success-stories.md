## Conclusions

- Always match story to domain. Never use generic "our students do well."
- Stories need: who they were → what they built/did → concrete outcome
- Lead with role/background match first, then outcome
- Salary numbers (12 LPA, 9 LPA, 120% hike, 110% hike, 90% hike) work for Indian market leads
- Company names (T-Series, ShopOS, Emergent, Malpani Group, TVF, Wenesday Solutions, Comprint) add credibility
- For no-code leads: Sanyam Narang, Hasti Sanghavi, Nishant Pandita, Manthan Maheshwari prove no-code works
- For complete career changers: Ayush Kejriwal (Psychologist → AI Engineer) is the strongest story
