# Sales Wiki — Overview

## What This Wiki Is For

This is the 100xEngineers sales team's second brain. It helps reps sell the 6-month Applied AI cohort to any kind of lead — engineers, designers, marketers, founders, doctors, animators, game designers, managers, VCs, and anyone else.

It provides **nuances, framings, facts, and domain intelligence** — not scripts. Reps use this to make a better case in their own voice.

## The Three Moments

**Pre-call:** Given lead's name, role, domain, years of experience → get call brief: best angle, likely objection, success story to have ready, discovery questions to ask.

**Mid-call:** Rep describes what the lead said → get domain-specific cohort relevance, objection framing, matched success story. Claude combines wiki knowledge + live web search for any domain.

**Post-call:** Rep describes call outcome + lead profile → draft personalized WhatsApp follow-up message in 100x voice.

## The Sales Philosophy (from Zahid)

> "My goal is not to convince them. My goal is to make them confront their reality. Once that happens, the sale becomes natural."

- Discovery before pitch, always
- Domain mirroring creates instant credibility
- The experience reframe removes "I'm not technical enough" fear
- FOMO through analogy (IT wave → AI wave), never pressure
- Matched success stories, never generic testimonials
- Never close on call 1 — the follow-up system converts
