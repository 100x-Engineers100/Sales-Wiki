## Summary

Internal 100xEngineers sales document covering 9 follow-up and objection scenarios. Each scenario has three response modes: Warm (low pressure, invites reflection), Pressure (forces clarity), and Straightforward (direct). Core philosophy: don't convince — make them reflect. Move logic → emotion → action.

## Key Ideas

- Three-mode system: Warm, Pressure, Straightforward. Rep picks based on reading the lead's energy.
- The goal is NEVER to sell the course. The goal is to make them confront their reality.
- Price objection is almost always about perceived value or lack of confidence — not actual affordability.
- "I'll think about it" almost always means "there's a specific doubt I haven't said out loud."
- "I'm busy" almost always means "it's not a priority right now."
- The strongest closing move is NOT pressure — it's asking the right question to surface the real reason.

## Notable Quotes / Moments

> "Is the issue price, or confidence in your ability to put in effort for the next 6 months?"

> "Most people who say 'later' end up not doing it — what's the real reason?"

> "Right now it feels like you want this, but you're not acting on it — what's the gap?"

> "Once that happens [confronting reality], the sale becomes natural."
