## Summary

Two real sales calls by Zahid (CRO and co-founder of 100xEngineers). Call 1 is with Harsh — a business analyst in software consulting, 4 years exp, general AI curiosity. Call 2 is with Rohit — a media agency founder, 8 years industry experience, already experimenting with AI. Both calls follow a clear pattern: permission-based opening → discovery → domain mirroring → experience reframe → FOMO → matched success story → cohort pitch → handoff. Neither call ends with a close — both end with "Anup will follow up."

## Key Ideas

- Discovery always precedes pitch. Zahid never pitches before understanding the person's role and goals.
- Domain mirroring is his most powerful live technique — he immediately connects 100x's sister companies or real use cases to the prospect's exact world.
- The experience reframe ("domain expertise + AI = the right combination") kills the "I'm not technical enough" fear without directly addressing it.
- FOMO is stated as genuine belief (IT wave vs AI wave), not as a closing tactic.
- Success stories are matched precisely to the prospect's background — never generic.
- Zahid never closes on call 1. The follow-up system (Anup + brochure + materials) handles conversion.
- Price is disclosed clearly and matter-of-factly: ₹1,18,490, no-cost EMI, 300hr GPU credits.

## Notable Quotes / Moments

> "People who've worked 4-5 years know what real problems look like. A college student has no idea. When AI knowledge comes along with that domain experience — that combination is where real impact happens."

> "AI in videos is not playing strengths — it's hiding weaknesses. Only once you use it, you'll realize what's possible."

> "My goal is not to convince them. My goal is to make them confront their reality."
